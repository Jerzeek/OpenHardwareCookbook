Raspberry Pi(e)

Submitted by Norman Lorrain (credit to my late mom, Thérèse) from Edmonton, Canada
Of course you were expecting this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup water, boiling
  • 1/2 cup water, cold
  • small box (85g) raspberry
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 4 cups frozen raspberries (fresh is OK, frozen are even better)
  • 1 pre-baked pie shell (see ), see

Directions

  1. Stir boiling water into Jell-O powder, until completely dissolved.
  2. Stir in sugar.
  3. Stir cold water.
  4. When cooled, add in raspberries.
  5. Chill until partially gelled (otherwise the liquid soaks into the shell).
  6. Add mixture to pie shell.
  7. Chill in refrigerator.
  8. Top with whipped cream.
